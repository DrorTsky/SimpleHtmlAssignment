Prague

          
 Prague, capital city of the Czech Republic, is bisected by the Vltava River. 
 Nicknamed “the City of a Hundred Spires,” it's known for its Old Town Square, 
 the heart of its historic core, with colorful baroque buildings, Gothic churches 
 and the medieval Astronomical Clock, which gives an animated hourly show.
