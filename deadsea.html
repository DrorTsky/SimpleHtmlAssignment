Dead sea

          
 The Dead Sea is one of the saltiest bodies of water on Earth, 
 with almost 10 times more salt than ordinary seawater. 
 This is because water flows into the Dead Sea from one main tributary, the River Jordan. 
 The high salt and mineral content of the Dead Sea mean that this body of water has powerful healing properties.
