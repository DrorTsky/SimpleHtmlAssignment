Grand Canyon

 
      The Grand Canyon of the Colorado River is a mile-deep canyon (1.6 km) that
      bisects the park. Even though the average distance across the canyon is
      only 10 miles/ 16 km, it takes 5 hours to drive the 215 miles / 346 km
      between the park's South Rim Village and the North Rim Village
